Refunds Policy

Thank you for choosing "War Plane: Mission Rescue" as your virtual reality gaming experience. We strive to provide our players with the best gaming experience possible. However, we understand that sometimes circumstances may arise that require a refund. Please review our refunds policy below:

1. Eligibility for Refunds:

  • Refunds are eligible for purchases made directly through the Oculus Quest store.
  • Refunds may be requested within 14 days of the purchase date, provided that you have not played the game for more than 2 hours.

2. Refund Process:

  • To request a refund, please contact Oculus Support directly through their website or app.
  • Refunds are subject to the approval of Oculus Support and may take some time to process.

3. Exclusions:

  • Refunds will not be provided for purchases made outside of the Oculus Quest store.
  • Refunds will not be provided for purchases made more than 14 days prior.
  • Refunds will not be provided for purchases where the game has been played for more than 2 hours.

4. Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding refunds or our refunds policy, please don't hesitate to contact us at contact@hypergames.store.

Please note that this refunds policy is subject to change without prior notice. We appreciate your understanding and support.
